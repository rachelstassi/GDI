Definitions of Prohibited Conduct


The following modules give additional information on conduct prohibited at PSU, specifically:

  • Gender/Sex Discrimination,
  • Discriminatory Harassment,
  • Sexual Harassment,
  • Sexual Misconduct,
  • Stalking,
  • Domestic and Dating Violence,
  • Sexual Exploitation, and
  • Retaliation.

After the definition, you will see an example of the issue. The questions following the scenarios serve as the quiz to complete the training. You will be able to move to the next module once you get the correct answer.
