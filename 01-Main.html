Creating a Safe, Respectful and Inclusive Campus

Welcome to Portland State University

At PSU, we are committed to fostering a campus community that prioritizes the health, safety, equity and well-being of our students. We want to provide you all the information you may need in case you or a friend have experienced gender discrimination, sexual harassment, sexual assault, or other concerns regarding sexual misconduct. If you need any accommodations in completing the training, please email saveact@pdx.edu to obtain a text version of the training.

The material in the training may be sensitive for some students. If you have questions or concerns about completing the training, please visit the Safe Campus website for more information or send an email to saveact@pdx.edu. If you would like support around any issues in the training, please contact the support services listed here. Also, please print this resource page for services available at PSU or off campus.

This training addresses the following topics:

  • Understanding what conduct is prohibited as defined by PSU policies and Student Code of Conduct, including:
    • Gender/Sex Discrimination
    • Retaliation
    • Sexual Harrassment
    • Sexual Assault, and other sexual misconduct policies
  • Where to go for help on and off campus
  • Who on campus can you talk to confidentially
  • How to report incidents of discrimination, harassment or sexual misconduct
    • Your Student Rights and Responsibilities
  • Bystander Intervention

Even if you are not directly affected by discrimination, sexual harassment, or sexual assault, chances are you know someone who will be or has been. It’s important that everyone learns about these issues. So you can know what conduct is prohibited and supported by PSU’s campus community to create a safer campus for everyone. The training is not meant to reflect on any individual, group or organization.

The training includes both information and sample situations and will test you on your understanding of the material as you work through the training and questions from the examples. You must answer correctly before you can move on. At the end, you will be asked if you completed the training and to obtain credit, you will need to answer yes. You may not skip pages to complete the training but you can always pause and return to where you left.
